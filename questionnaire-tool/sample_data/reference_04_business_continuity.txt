Business Continuity Plan v1.4

AlmaLedger maintains a documented disaster recovery plan with Recovery Time Objective (RTO) of 8 hours and Recovery Point Objective (RPO) of 1 hour for critical services.
High availability is maintained through multi-zone deployment in the primary region.
Business continuity testing is performed once per year, and findings are tracked to closure.
On-call operations follow a 24x7 rotation for production incidents.
